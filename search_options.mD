## 💡 Tips for Searching

1. **Start broad, then narrow.** Search by classification first (e.g. *Paintings*), then add a culture or medium to refine.
2. **Capitalization matters in display, not in search.** The API is case-insensitive, but proper capitalization makes your results easier to read.
3. **Combine fields creatively.** Try *Culture: Japanese* + *Technique: Woodblock print* for ukiyo-e prints, or *Classification: Sculpture* + *Medium: Bronze* + *Culture: Greek* for classical bronzes.
4. **Use keywords for themes.** If you want a *love*-themed exhibition, try the keyword field rather than guessing classifications.
